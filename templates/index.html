Pneumonia Detection

About

This app uses an ensemble of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) to predict if a given chest X-ray image shows signs of pneumonia. It can be used as a tool to assist doctors and radiologists in their diagnosis.

How to Use

  1. Upload a chest X-ray image in JPEG or PNG format.
  2. Click on the "Predict" button to get the prediction result.
  3. Read the disclaimer before submitting.

Disclaimer

  1. The predictions made by the model are not guaranteed to be accurate.
  2. The model is not intended to replace professional opinions or medical advice.
